Sales forecasting using machine learning involves using statistical models and algorithms to analyze historical sales data and predict future sales trends. This approach can provide valuable insights into consumer behavior, market trends, and potential business opportunities. One popular machine learning library used for time series forecasting is the Prophet library developed by Facebook, which we have implemented in this model. It uses Bayesian methods to model time series data, incorporating trend, seasonality, and holiday effects. Prophet is designed to be highly customizable and easy to use, making it accessible to both novice and experienced data scientists. Its flexibility and scalability have made it a popular choice for businesses and researchers alike.
