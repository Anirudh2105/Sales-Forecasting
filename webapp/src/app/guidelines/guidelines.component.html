GUIDELINES FOR USING PREDICT IT

  • 1. Upload your file (in csv or excel format )
  • 2. Select the Periodicity (Frequency of recurring events over time, i.e daily,monthly.. )
  • 3. Select the Numerix (The numerics related periodicity)
  • 4. Upload
  • 5. See your prediction graph
  • 6. Click on trends to see the seasonality trends
  • 7. Also you can see the predicted data sets
  • 8. Enjoy your prediction :)
